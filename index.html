Challenges We Address

  • Lack of digital tools to keep track of emissions related to food consumption.
  • Difficulty for climate-conscious restaurants to connect with potential customers and vice versa.
  • Greenwashing undermining trust in sustainability efforts.
  • While nutrition scores for food products are mainstream, this information is often missing in the food service landscape.
